Okabe Rintaro

Rintaro Okabe (岡部 倫太郎, Okabe Rintarō?), often nicknamed Okarin (オカリン) or Hououin Kyouma (鳳凰院凶真, Hououin Kyōma), is a self-proclaimed mad scientist and the main protagonist of the Steins;Gate series. Okabe has the characteristics of a lanky young man, with a square jaw and bony cheeks. He has his short black hair swept back and has brown irises.

Makise Kurisu

Kurisu Makise (牧瀬 紅莉栖 Makise Kurisu?) is the daughter of Shouichi Makise, a genius girl who graduated from university at the age of seventeen, and a member of the Brain Science Institute at Viktor Chondria University and the Future Gadget Lab. Kurisu is one of the main characters of Steins;Gate and a major posthumous character in Steins;Gate 0 via Amadeus. She's generally a sensible and tsundere.

Shiina Mayuri

Mayuri Shiina (椎名 まゆり, Shīna Mayuri), also known as Mayushii☆, is one of the main characters of Steins;Gate and Steins;Gate 0. She is Rintaro Okabe's childhood friend, a part-time worker at May Queen Nyan-nyan and a member of the Future Gadget Lab. She is also an avid cosplay maker and friend of Luka Urushibara and Akiha Rumiho in Steins;Gate 1 and 0.

Hashida Itaru

Itaru Hashida (橋田 至, Hashida Itaru), also known by his nickname Daru, his online handle DaSH ("Daru the Super Hacker") and Barrel Titor, is a main character in Steins;Gate and Steins;Gate 0. He is an experienced hacker and a hardcore otaku, a member of the Future Gadget Lab and a close friend of Rintaro Okabe who eventually becomes the husband of Yuki Amane.
